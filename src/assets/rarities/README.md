# Rarity art drop-in — slot reel 2

Put PNGs named `common.png`, `rare.png`, `epic.png`, `legendary.png` here and
the RARITY reel shows them automatically instead of the procedural gem.

- Recommended size: ~140×112 or larger with transparent background; images are
  contain-fitted to roughly 70×56 inside the reel cell, with the rarity label
  kept underneath.
- Rarities without art keep the procedural gem.
